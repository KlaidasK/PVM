Wood carving automation

Team description:

The Wood Carving Automation team is focused on revolutionizing traditional wood carving methods by integrating modern automation technologies. Our goal is to create a smart, programmable system that can craft intricate designs on wood surfaces with precision and efficiency. By leveraging robotic arms, CNC (Computer Numerical Control) systems, and advanced software algorithms, we aim to streamline the process of wood carving, making it more accessible and less labor-intensive for artisans and manufacturers alike. This project will explore various automation techniques while maintaining the authenticity of handcrafted designs, offering a perfect blend of artistry and technology. We're looking for innovative thinkers and engineers who can push the boundaries of traditional craftsmanship through cutting-edge automation solutions.
